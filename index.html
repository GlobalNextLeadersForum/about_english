About Us
  • GLOBAL NEXT LEADERS FORUM = GNLF

    • The Global Next Leaders Forum is an international project centered on an international conference that brings together leading university students and professors from around the world.

    Once a year, we hold a plenary session that invites participants from about 10 countries and regions, providing an opportunity to interact with a variety of people who have grown up in environments different from our own. The theme and program for the 2022 plenary session can be found here.
